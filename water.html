Don't drop the water

Water is a scarce and finite resource, is necessary for life. 71% of our earth is covered in water, only 3% of it is fresh water. Out of that 3%, only 1% is available, the remaining 2% being locked in glaciers and ice caps. Water is not infinite as you can see now, that's the reason is important to less use water.

Also, water is fishes home, under the sea, laggons and rivers are a lot of fishes and different water animals who need the water to have a nice place to life.

Taking showers less than 15 minutes, don't let the water dropping while you are brushing your teeth is a perfect ways to save water.
